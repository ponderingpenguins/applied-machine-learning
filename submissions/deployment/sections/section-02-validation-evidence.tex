\section{Validation Evidence}

The Transformer model reaches a validation equal error rate (EER) of 7.68\%. For a random baseline, the EER level would be 50\%, which is randomly choosing to accept or reject a given embedding. Here we see that the model is clearly better than random guessing on the validation data. Its important to note that because this task is verification rather than plain multiclass classification, EER is the relevant metric to report, such that lower is better. $(7.68\% \ll 50\%)$
